You are evaluating whether a Canadian education program (CIP) is an educational pathway into a Canadian occupation (NOC).

The goal is to judge real career alignment — not general skill similarity.

-------------------------------------

TASK
Decide how well completing the CIP prepares someone to ENTER the NOC occupation.

A true pathway means the program is normally intended to lead into that profession,
possibly followed by standard post-graduation steps (licensing, residency, apprenticeship, certification, supervised experience).

-------------------------------------

IMPORTANT PRINCIPLE
Be conservative.

Only choose YES when the CIP is a typical or standard entry route into the occupation.

If the fields are merely related, adjacent, or transferable,
the answer should be LEAN_YES, LEAN_NO, or NO — not YES.

When uncertain, prefer the lower label rather than the higher one.

(False positives are worse than false negatives.)

-------------------------------------

ALLOWED LABELS (choose exactly one)

YES
The CIP is a common direct preparation for this occupation.

LEAN_YES
Strongly related field and plausibly leads into the occupation,
but not a standard or primary pathway.

UNSURE
Insufficient or ambiguous information to determine alignment.

LEAN_NO
Some overlap exists but substantial retraining or a different specialization is required.

NO
Different profession or unrealistic pathway.

-------------------------------------

DECISION GUIDANCE

Consider:
• what the program trains people to become
• the occupation’s requirements and duties
• level expectations (trade, diploma, bachelor, professional degree, residency, etc.)
• professional identity (what job the graduate is meant to pursue)

Generic abilities (communication, analysis, teamwork, research skills) NEVER justify YES or LEAN_YES.

-------------------------------------

CONFIDENCE

Confidence reflects strength of evidence and must follow these ranges:

YES ≥ 0.80
LEAN_YES 0.60–0.79
UNSURE 0.40–0.59
LEAN_NO 0.20–0.39
NO ≤ 0.19

Do not reuse the same confidence repeatedly.
Choose values that reflect strength of match.

-------------------------------------

RATIONALE REQUIREMENTS

1–2 concise sentences.

Must reference:
• one concrete CIP detail
• one NOC requirement or duty

Do not rely on generic skills.

-------------------------------------

OUTPUT FORMAT

Return ONLY valid JSON with exactly these keys:

label
confidence
rationale
model_version
prompt_version

model_version: short string (e.g., "llama3:8b")
prompt_version: "{{prompt_version}}"
